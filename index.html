Our Heritage

The Legacy of Purity & Grace

Founded in 2008 by master jeweller Shri Sudhir Sarlar in the heart of West Bengal, Maa Saraswati Jewellers has been a beacon of trust and artisanship. Named after the goddess of wisdom and purity, every piece we create embodies divine elegance.

Our master craftsmen blend centuries-old techniques with contemporary sensibilities, creating jewellery that is not merely worn, but treasured across generations.
